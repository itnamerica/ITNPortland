Fine Rides for Fine Arts

Join the Culture of Kindness.
Become an ITN volunteer driver and help older people enjoy the arts.

With a grant from the Rines Thompson Foundation, the Independent Transportation Network of Portland has joined with the Portland Stage Company and the Portland Symphony Orchestra to launch the Fine Rides for Fine Arts program to engage people who appreciate the arts in a volunteer driver program that helps older people attend concerts and plays.

Through ITNâ€™s Culture of Caring, volunteer drivers earn credits for driving. By donating their credits to a Road Scholarship Fund for the Fine Rides for Fine Arts program, they help older people who need help paying for rides enjoy a symphony or a play.

    As a volunteer driver for the Fine Rides for Fine Arts program, you will earn:
  • a free membership for a lower-income senior;
  • a $50 cash donation for the Fine Rides for Fine Arts program; and
  • transportation credits for all of your volunteer driver miles.
    Who is Fine Rides for Fine Arts for?
  • Are you looking for a pleasant way to meet new people and give back to the community?
    Become a volunteer driver.
  • Do you need a little help with transportation to concerts or plays?
    Sign up to become a member.
  • Do you need help with costs? Ask about the Fine Rides for Fine Arts Road Scholarship fund. Call ITN at 854-0505.
