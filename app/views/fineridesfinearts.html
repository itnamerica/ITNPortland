Fine Rides for Fine Arts

Join the Culture of Kindness, with the ITN Fine Rides for Fine Arts volunteer team!

Fine Rides for Fine Arts is a collaboration between ITNPortland, the Portland Symphony Orchestra and Portland Stage Company. It pairs volunteer drivers with older arts patrons who otherwise might have to give up their tickets to their favorite performances. The program improves access to the arts for older patrons, and at the same time creates a fund for older riders of limited means to ensure everyone has access to their favorite shows and performances.

Do you want to help others, keep your community arts organizations vibrant and spend your free timemeeting interesting new people? Become a volunteer driver for ITNPortland, and designate your credits to the Fine Rides for Fine Arts program.

    As a Fine Rides driver:
  • You’ll receive a free membership to ITNPortland for a lower-income senior.
  • You’re enrollment will earn a $50 cash donation for the Fine Rides for Fine Arts program.
  • Drive when you have free-time, and the benefit still goes to the Arts Organizations.

Every mile you drive will earn you transportation credits, which you can save for your own future transportation needs, share with someone else in need of transportation, or donate back to ITNPortland for riders in need.

Every Fine Rides trip not only supports the individual rider, but their patronage supports the arts organizations that keep this community vibrant. Join the Culture of Kindness: support your older neighbors while supporting the arts. Become part of Fine Rides for Fine Arts.
